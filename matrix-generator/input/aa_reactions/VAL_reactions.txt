3-Hydroxy-3-methyl-2-oxobutanoic acid + 1*NADH → (R)-2,3-Dihydroxy-3-methylbutanoate
(R)-2,3-Dihydroxy-3-methylbutanoate → 3-Mehtyl-2-oxobutanoic acid
3-Mehtyl-2-oxobutanoic acid + L-Glutamate → L-Valine + 2-Oxoglutarate
